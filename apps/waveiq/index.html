Radio discovery with more context

WaveIQ Radio combines internet radio playback with discovery tools for finding stations by name, genre, country, language, or natural prompts.

Play and save stations

Listen to internet radio, preview stations before saving them, keep saved stations and favorites, and use station cards with logos or consistent placeholders.

Search and browse

Browse and search stations by name, genre, country, or language, with AI-assisted station search from natural prompts.

Recommendations

Personalized recommendations use listening taste and station signals. Where enough station data is available, recommendation explanations can help explain why a station appears.

Similar stations

Discover alternatives to a station you already like, with tuning options for less talk, more energy, and calmer results.

Privacy and local-first summary

WaveIQ Radio keeps the core listening experience local-first: saved stations, favorites, settings, and station logos are stored on the device.

Online features such as AI search, recommendations, similar stations, stream health, and backup require network services.

Backup and restore

WaveIQ Radio supports backup and restore for saved stations, settings, and logos.

Google Drive backup is available through user-authorized Google sign-in, with manual backup/restore and automatic backup controls including Wi-Fi-only behavior.
